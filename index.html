Why Me?

A lot of people say they're passionate about aerospace. I build things. Before second year. Before anyone told me to.

I didn't wait for a class to teach me C++. I taught myself and built a functioning radar scanner.

I'm hungry in a way that doesn't switch off. My parents gave up everything to put me here and I treat every day like that means something.

There is an itch that never goes away. A curiosity that does not ask for permission. I do not wait to be assigned a problem · I find one, pick it apart, and build something from what is left. That drive is not something I learned. It is just the way I am wired.
